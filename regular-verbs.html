Verbs & Tenses

Regular verbs

Regular verbs add -ed for both the past simple and past participle. Boring in the best way — learn the spelling rules once, and they all behave.

Spelling rules for -ed

  • Most verbs: just add -ed. work → worked.
  • Ends in -e: add -d. live → lived.
  • Consonant + -y: change y → i, add -ed. study → studied.
  • Short verb, one vowel + one consonant: double the consonant. stop → stopped.

Pronunciation of -ed

Ending sound of verb-ed is pronouncedExamples
voiceless (p, k, f, s, sh, ch, x)/t/worked, stopped, washed
